About Orlando Terrace

Orlando Terrace is an apartment community that prioritizes convenience and your satisfaction. We offer studio, 1-bedroom, and 2-bedroom homes featuring private patios, vinyl plank flooring, and brand-new kitchen appliances. Located in vibrant Orlando, enjoy easy access to Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and UCF, all just minutes away. Apartments are available now and move-in requires no security deposit!
